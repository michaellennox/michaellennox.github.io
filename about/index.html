Hi, I'm Michael and I'm a Makers Graduate and Junior Developer

I'm a Makers Academy Graduate who's now working as a ruby developer in the big wide world.

Professional Background

Born and bred in Yorkshire, I traded the beautiful dales for London's urban sprawl to study Medicine at UCL. 18 months in, I came to the realisation that a life in the medical field wasn't for me and jumped into the working world

After leaving Medicine I entered a career in sales, first working as a team lead in a sales role for an agency in the energy industry and then taking up a Technical Recruitment role for the fantastic Explore Group - a mid-sized agency focused on the London market.

While at the Explore Group, I ran one of our open source language desks focused on the python community and had the priviledge of working with a wide variety of London's greatest names in technology. It truly opened my eyes to how remarkable the tech community in London is and ignited a keen desire to get into a position where instead of helping from the sidelines, I could become intimately involved.

After nearly 4 years of doing fairly well I decided it was time for a change and went on the fantastic Makers Academy program where they taught me all those lovely things: writing clean, reusable code, passion for quality testing, and of course, the ability to put together applications in a wide variety of languages and technologies.
